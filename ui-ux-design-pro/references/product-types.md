## Product-type heuristic
- Start from primary task frequency, not stakeholder wishlists.
- Match density to operator expertise and session length.
- Treat compliance-heavy products as evidence systems, not just dashboards.
- Separate browse, act, review, and monitor modes when workflows conflict.
